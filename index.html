📖 How to Use

1️⃣ Install the extension

Download from Chrome Web Store or load unpacked in Developer mode.

2️⃣ Copy anything

Select text on any page and press Ctrl+C – it’s automatically saved.

3️⃣ Open the popup

Click the extension icon to see your history, search, or re‑copy items.

4️⃣ Manage your history

Click a card to copy, use the dustbin icon to delete, or clear all with the button.

5️⃣ Toggle privacy modes

Use the per‑tab switch to ignore the current page, or the global switch to pause all capture.

6️⃣ Share feedback (optional)

When the feedback card appears, rate your experience and help us improve.

🛟 Support

Frequently Asked Questions
Is my data safe?+
Absolutely. All clipboard history is stored locally on your device. Nothing leaves your computer except voluntary feedback.
Can I disable the extension temporarily?+
Yes, use the global master switch in the popup – it stops all capture instantly without uninstalling.
Does it capture passwords?+
No, the extension never accesses password fields. It only captures plain text from regular pages and input areas.
How do I clear my history completely?+
Click the Clear All button and confirm. That wipes every saved clipboard item.
The extension isn’t capturing copies – what’s wrong?+
Ensure the global toggle is ON (green) and the per‑tab toggle is active. Also, try refreshing the page.
