Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

Your privacy is critically important to us. This policy explains how Smart Clipboard Manager ("the Extension") handles your data.

1. Data Collection & Storage

The Extension stores clipboard history locally on your device using chrome.storage.local. This includes:

  • Text you copy from web pages, input fields, and textareas.
  • Timestamps associated with each copied item.

We do not collect, transmit, or share this data with any external server. All history remains entirely on your machine.

2. Optional Feedback & Discord Integration

If you choose to submit feedback (after 50 uses or 7 days, via the in‑popup card), the following information is voluntarily sent to a Discord webhook:

  • Your star rating (1‑5).
  • Your feedback message.
  • An optional email address (only if you provide one).
  • Browser information (user agent).
  • Extension version.
  • Submission timestamp.

This data is used solely to improve the Extension and is stored on Discord's servers (see Discord's privacy policy). No other data is shared.

3. Permissions Justification

The Extension requires the following permissions for full functionality:

  • storage – to save clipboard history locally.
  • clipboardRead – to read the current system clipboard when you click "Import from clipboard".
  • clipboardWrite – to copy saved items back to your clipboard.
  • offscreen – (optional) to keep the background service worker alive for uninterrupted capture.
  • host_permissions <all_urls> – to detect copy events on any website you visit.
  • activeTab – to enable/disable capture on the current tab.

None of these permissions are used to track or monitor your browsing activity.

4. Your Control Over Data

  • Delete individual items – click the 🗑️ button on any history card.
  • Clear all history – use the "Clear All" button with confirmation.
  • Disable capture per tab – toggle off the current tab in the popup.
  • Global master switch – instantly stop all clipboard capturing across all tabs.

All controls are available directly in the Extension popup.

5. Data Retention

Clipboard history is stored indefinitely until you delete it manually or clear all history. Feedback submissions are retained on Discord as per Discord's data retention policies.

6. Third‑Party Services

The Extension uses Discord solely for optional feedback collection. Discord's privacy policy applies to any data you submit through that channel. No other third‑party services, analytics, or advertising networks are used.

7. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
